Mom's Chili

This chili is based off the classic Better Homes and Gardens chili. After making it for so many years, I've made some changes that I think greatly improve the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Kidney Beans
  • Bell Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Diced Tomato

Steps

  • Cook ground beef, onion, garlic, and pepper in the bottom of your pot.
