Grilled Cheese with Apples and Honey Butter

The sweet and salty combination of this sandwich makes it feel absolutely extravagant to eat, despite it being such a simple recipe! Prep time can be reduced by pre-cutting apples, keeping softened butter, and mixing salad dressing ahead of time.

Ingredients

  • Sharp Cheddar Cheese (or cheese of your choice)
  • Choice of Apple (I like Honeycrisp or Pink Lady apples for this recipe)
  • Sourdough Bread
  • Butter
  • Honey
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  • Slice enough cheese to fit on your bread.
  • Slice apples to fit bread.
  • Mix softened butter with honey until it's well blended and easy to spread.
  • Heat a skillet up on the stove.
  • Spread your honey butter on the outside AND inside of your first piece of bread.
  • Lay buttered bread in the skillet, quickly layering cheese and apples.
  • Quickly spread honey butter on both sides of your top piece of bread and add it.
  • Flip your sandwich carefully once you see the cheese starting to soften.
